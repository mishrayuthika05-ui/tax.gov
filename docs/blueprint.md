# **App Name**: e-Tax Sahayak

## Core Features:

- Taxpayer Identification and Verification: Securely identify and verify taxpayers using government-issued IDs (e.g., Aadhaar, PAN) and integrate with existing databases for validation.
- Automated Tax Calculation: Calculate tax liabilities based on income, deductions, and applicable tax laws for various taxpayer categories (individuals, businesses, etc.).
- AI-Driven Audit Selection: Employ an AI tool to analyze tax returns and identify high-risk cases for audit based on anomalies, patterns, and compliance history.
- Online Tax Filing: Enable taxpayers to file their returns online through a user-friendly interface with pre-filled information and secure data transmission.
- Automated Notices and Communication: Generate and send automated notices, reminders, and communication to taxpayers regarding deadlines, payments, and compliance requirements.
- Real-time Payment Processing: Integrate with payment gateways to facilitate secure and real-time processing of tax payments through various channels (net banking, credit/debit cards, UPI, etc.).
- Data Analytics and Reporting: Provide data analytics and reporting capabilities to track tax revenue, compliance rates, and identify areas for improvement in tax policy and administration.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep saffron (#FF9933) to evoke a sense of national identity and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), providing a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Navy blue (#000080) for important actions and links, conveying authority and reliability.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a modern humanist sans-serif font for a clean, accessible look suitable for both headings and body text.
- Use simple, line-based icons to represent different tax categories and actions, ensuring clarity and ease of understanding.
- Design a clear and intuitive layout with a focus on usability, ensuring that taxpayers can easily navigate the system and complete their tasks efficiently.
- Implement subtle animations to guide users through the tax filing process and provide feedback on their actions, enhancing the overall user experience.
